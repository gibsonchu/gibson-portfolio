About

Hello there!

Recently, I've stepped into the wonderful world of freelance, actively challenging myself with personal projects and reaching out to clients! With a love for storytelling, I specialize in portraiture and lifestyle work, dabbling in travel, landscape and product.

My 2019 goal is to begin developing an online presence and growing my portfolio by sharing my experiences through Instagram and this website. I hope to extend this into wedding photography and other content creators/companies down the road.

Feel free to reach out at gibsontchu@gmail.com or shooting a DM over on my Insta. Check out my other non-photography work at gibsonchu.me.
